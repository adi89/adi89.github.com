The Resume

It's pretty interesting. I only graduated a year ago. I earned a Biomedical Engineering degree, something I had chased since high school, but in senior year I had my mind set on something else. I want to build things. As crass as it sounds, I want to put my name on something and have the satifcation of having gotten out what I put in. So I knew what I had to do. I put my nose to the grindstone and got to work. After school, I worked as an Inspector at the Battery Maritime Building post-Sandy. It was a hard time having to manage myself and learn the intricacies of the counterweight drawbridge system. I did so with the image of pursuing Web Development in the forefront of my mind. I showed up a fish out of water at Web Dev Immersive, but I've really made strides at improving. I've came a long ways in short while, but I've just gotten started.
